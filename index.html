ABOUT US

Brazilian Jiu Jitsu 3rd degree black belt Steve Campbell is the head instructor at Alavanca BJJ. Our team focuses on BJJ classes for the complete beginner as well as advanced students. This way, new starters are always joining and becoming valuable training partners for the future. To achieve this goal, we developed our BJJ beginners programme. This is a structured, progressive and fun syllabus enabling students to train in a safe environment. This approach means everyone can excel regardless of height, weight or athletic ability.
Brazilian Jiu Jitsu is an effective form of self-defense made famous by the Gracie family in early UFC events. First and foremost it is a submission grappling style based around correct technique and leverage. The fundamental principle is to allow a smaller person to overcome a larger stronger opponent.
Our Brazilian Jiu Jitsu classes based near Oldham are a great way to improve your fitness, lose weight and gain self confidence. These jiu jitsu skills then enable students to defend themselves in case a life-threatening situation arises.
